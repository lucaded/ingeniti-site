The grid’s biggest untapped asset is stranded.

Billions of devices sit disconnected from any unified control plane. An enormous pool of latent grid capacity. Every utility is running the same play against the same wall: custom point product deployments that cost too much, take too long, and fragment on the first integration. Meanwhile, the grid keeps getting more stressed.

The Solution

One platform for every device. Every program.

OPTIMA is one platform that connects every electrical device on the grid. EV chargers at a fleet depot, HVAC in a hospital, solar panels on a home, batteries in a microgrid. Different brands, different protocols, all monitored, controlled, and optimized from a single place.

Utilities run their full grid coordination on OPTIMA instead of stitching together dozens of vendor systems. Consumers see and control every device on their site, and can opt in to grid programs that pay them back. What used to take years and millions of dollars to deploy now ships in months at a fraction of the cost.
